Helplines & Crisis Support

If you or someone you know is struggling, please reach out for help. You are not alone — there are people ready to listen and support you 24/7.

Call 988 Now

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline (U.S.)

Call or Text: 988

Website: 988lifeline.org

Crisis Text Line

Text: 741741 (U.S. & Canada)

Website: crisistextline.org

The Trevor Project (LGBTQ+ Youth)

Call: 866-488-7386

Text: 678-678

Website: thetrevorproject.org

Hey Sam (Peer Text Line)

Text: 439-726 (U.S. only)

Website: samaritanshope.org/our-services/hey-sam

Veterans Crisis Line

Call: 988, then press 1

Text: 838255

Website: veteranscrisisline.net

International Helplines

Find local hotlines and mental health services by country.

Website: findahelpline.com

This page is for educational awareness as part of a student project. If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a crisis, please contact emergency services or one of the helplines above.
